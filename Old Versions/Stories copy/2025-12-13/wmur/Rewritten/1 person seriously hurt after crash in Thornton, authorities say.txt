Title: Serious Injuries Reported Following Collision in Thornton

One individual sustained severe injuries in a collision that occurred in Thornton on Friday evening. Emergency services were notified just prior to 7:30 p.m. regarding an accident on Interstate 93 South, where a person was reported to be trapped. Rescue teams required approximately 20 minutes to extricate the individual, who was subsequently transported to a nearby hospital. Authorities from the State Police are currently investigating the circumstances surrounding the incident.
