Annual Church Street tree lighting rings in the Christmas season

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-11-29
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/11/29/annual-church-street-tree-lighting-rings-christmas-season/

BURLINGTON, Vt. (WCAX) - It’s beginning to look a lot like Christmas in the Queen City.

People are really getting into the holiday spirit on church street as the lights on the Burlington Christmas tree shine once again.

As Channel 3’s Abigail Saxe saw, despite the cold, lots of people made it out for the fun.
