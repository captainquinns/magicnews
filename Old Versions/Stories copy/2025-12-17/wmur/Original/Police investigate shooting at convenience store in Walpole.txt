Police investigate shooting at convenience store in Walpole

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/shooting-jiffy-mart-walpole-121725/69799287

Police in Walpole were investigating a shooting Wednesday at a Jiffy Mart on Main Street.

There was no immediate word on any injuries.

Police said everyone involved in the shooting is accounted for, and they don't believe there is any threat to the public.
