Plane crashes at residential complex in Nashua

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/nashua-new-hampshire-plane-crash-121725/69800373

A plane crashed in Nashua on Wednesday afternoon.

The plane crashed at a residential complex in the area of Cannongate Road, which is on the other side of Route 101A from Nashua Airport.

The crash happened before 2:30 p.m.

An image from the scene shows a small plane upside down outside the row of homes. The image also shows apparent damage to the roof of one of the homes.

According to Nashua police, the pilot was the only person on board the plane at the time of the crash.

The extent of injuries, if any, was not immediately known.
