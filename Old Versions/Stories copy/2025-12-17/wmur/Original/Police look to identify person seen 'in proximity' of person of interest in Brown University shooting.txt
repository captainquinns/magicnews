Police look to identify person seen 'in proximity' of person of interest in Brown University shooting

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/brown-university-shooting-investigation/69799711

Police in Providence, Rhode Island, are looking to identify a person seen "in proximity" of the person of interest in the shooting at Brown University on Saturday.

>> UPDATE: A news conference is scheduled for 4 p.m. Wednesday. See it streaming live in the video player above at that time. <<

A gunman opened fire Saturday afternoon inside a classroom in the Barus and Holley building, which houses the School of Engineering and physics department. Two students were killed, and nine others were injured.

Investigators on Tuesday released new video showing the person of interest's movements near the shooting scene before and after the shooting.
