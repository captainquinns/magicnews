Trial involving Market Basket leadership feud begins

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/market-basket-trial-begins/69796893

A trial involving the Market Basket Board of Directors and ousted CEO Arthur T. Demoulas is now underway. The trial started Tuesday in Delaware.

A judge will decide whether Demoulas was improperly fired by his three sisters earlier this year.

Demoulas claims his firing happened before he had the opportunity to defend himself.
