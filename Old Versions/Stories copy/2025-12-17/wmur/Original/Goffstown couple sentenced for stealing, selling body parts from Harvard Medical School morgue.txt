Goffstown couple sentenced for stealing, selling body parts from Harvard Medical School morgue

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/cedric-lodge-denise-lodge-sentenced-12172025/69792461

Two people from New Hampshire have been sentenced for their role in stealing and selling human remains from the Harvard Medical School morgue.

Cedric Lodge of Goffstown, the former manager of the morgue, pleaded guilty to interstate transport of state goods.

He was sentenced to eight years in prison.

His wife, Denise Lodge, sold the remains to buyers across the country.

She was sentenced to a year in prison.
