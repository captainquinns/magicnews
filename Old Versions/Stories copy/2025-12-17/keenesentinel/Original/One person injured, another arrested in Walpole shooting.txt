One person injured, another arrested in Walpole shooting

Site: KEENESENTINEL
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.keenesentinel.com/news/local/walpole-nh-december-2025-shooting/article_1e4f3a9d-6d46-413a-8ff4-cb299bc28a8b.html

WALPOLE — One person was taken into custody and another was injured Wednesday morning in a shooting on Main Street, police said.

The shooting, which was reported around 10:45 a.m. at the Jiffy Mart convenience store and gas station on Main Street, was an isolated incident, according to Walpole Police Chief Justin Sanctuary. He said there was no danger to the public.

The victim sustained a single gunshot wound to the torso, according to Sanctuary. He said the victim was taken by ambulance to Cheshire Medical Center in Keene and then by helicopter to Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical Center in Lebanon.

The alleged shooter was arrested without incident, according to Sanctuary. He did not provide the names of the people involved, but said both are adult men and the victim is a Walpole resident.

The convenience store and gas station where the shooting occured will remain closed for the rest of the day, he added.

Sanctuary said N.H. State Police is assisting with the investigation.

In a Wednesday afternoon Facebook post, Walpole police said more information will be shared later about this incident.

This is a developing story. More information will be added as it becomes available.
