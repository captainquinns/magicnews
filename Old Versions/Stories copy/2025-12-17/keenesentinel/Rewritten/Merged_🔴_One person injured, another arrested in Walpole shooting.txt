**Headline: One Injured in Walpole Shooting Incident; Investigation Underway**

 

Walpole, New Hampshire, witnessed a shooting incident Wednesday morning at the Jiffy Mart convenience store on Main Street. The event, which was reported around 10:45 a.m., resulted in one person suffering a gunshot wound and another being arrested. Law enforcement officials, including Walpole Police and New Hampshire State Police, responded promptly, establishing a perimeter around the location as they investigated the scene.

The victim, identified as an adult male resident of Walpole, was initially taken by ambulance to Cheshire Medical Center in Keene due to a traumatic injury. He was later airlifted to Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical Center in Lebanon for further treatment. Details regarding the specific nature of his injuries have not been disclosed.

Authorities indicated that the alleged shooter was apprehended without incident on site. Police Chief Justin Sanctuary affirmed that the incident was isolated and posed no ongoing threat to the public, a sentiment echoed in communications from the Walpole Police Department and state officials.

Photographs taken at the scene captured a red pickup truck with a fuel pump still connected and another blue pickup truck, both of which were part of the ongoing investigation. The Jiffy Mart has since been closed for the remainder of the day as police continue their inquiries.

This situation remains fluid, with reports suggesting that more information will be released as the investigation progresses. As of now, all individuals involved have been accounted for, and safety measures are in place to ensure public security. Further updates will be provided as they become available.
