Truck attempting U-turn ruptures propane tank in Shaftsbury

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-01
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/01/vermont-state-police-investigating-propane-leak-after-tractor-trailer-hit-it/

SHAFTSBURY, Vt. (WCAX) - Police are investigating after they say a tractor-trailer caused a propane tank leak in Shaftsbury.

Troopers say it happened along Vt. Route 7A when a tractor-trailer unit attempted to make a U-turn on the front lawn of a property, damaging a propane tank located underground and causing the tank to leak.

State police and local fire and emergency personnel responded. They secured the area and worked to contain the leak.

No one was injured.

The incident is still under investigation. Anyone with information is asked to call the state police in Shaftsbury at 802-442-5421.
