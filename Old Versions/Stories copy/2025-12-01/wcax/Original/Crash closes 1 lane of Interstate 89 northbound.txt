Crash closes 1 lane of Interstate 89 northbound

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-01
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/01/crash-closes-1-lane-interstate-89-northbound/

COLCHESTER, Vt. (WCAX) - A traffic alert for drivers-- police said Monday afternoon that one lane of northbound Interstate 89 in Colchester was closed because of a crash.

Officials said at about 3:30 p.m. that the closure could last for an hour or two.

They warned drivers to expect delays or find other routes.
