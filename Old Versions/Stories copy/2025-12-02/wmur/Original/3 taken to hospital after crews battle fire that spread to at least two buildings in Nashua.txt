3 taken to hospital after crews battle fire that spread to at least two buildings in Nashua

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-02
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/nashua-new-hampshire-vine-street-4-alarm-fire-reported/69604668

A fire spread through at least two buildings in Nashua on Tuesday morning.

The fire started just before 6:30 a.m. at buildings between Ash and Vine streets.

An officer at the scene told WMUR that the fire broke out at a six-family home at 108 Ash St. and spread to the warehouse at 115 Vine St.

Police said officers worked with firefighters to evacuate the apartment building. Police said they believe they got everyone out.

Officials said three civilians were transported to local hospitals to receive treatment for injuries that were not deemed life-threatening.
