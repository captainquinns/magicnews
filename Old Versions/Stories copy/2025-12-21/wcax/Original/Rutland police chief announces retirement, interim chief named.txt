Rutland police chief announces retirement, interim chief named

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-21
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/21/rutland-police-chief-announces-retirement-interim-chief-named/

RUTLAND, Vt. (WCAX) - Rutland police chief Brian Kilcullen is set to retire.

The commission accepted Kilcullen’s retirement notice and appointed Interim Chief Matt Prouty, effective immediately. Prouty will assume full authorities and responsibilities of the chief’s office, including operational oversight of the department.

Kilcullen provided formal notice of his intent to retire Friday. He will remain with the department through March 1 in a limited capacity to support the transition.

“The Rutland City Police Commission is committed to maintaining stability and uninterrupted service to our community,” the Commission stated. “This transition plan ensures clear leadership, operational continuity, and a structured handoff of responsibilities.”
