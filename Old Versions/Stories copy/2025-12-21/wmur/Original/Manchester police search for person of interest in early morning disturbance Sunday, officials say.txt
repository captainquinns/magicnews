Manchester police search for person of interest in early morning disturbance Sunday, officials say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-21
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/manchester-police-search-for-person-of-interest-12212025/69830471

Manchester Police are asking for assistance after a disturbance at the Manchester Police Department lobby Sunday.

Officials say a man entered the Manchester Police Department lobby and stated he was going to hurt someone and appeared angry.

The man then left the lobby before he could be identified.

Police have now identified and located the man, officials say.
