Holiday parade in Fremont cut short due to second-alarm fire in nearby home, officials say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-21
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/fremont-holiday-parade-cut-short-after-fire-12212025/69830379

A holiday parade in Fremont Saturday was cut short because of a second-alarm fire.

Fire crews say they were able to get to the fire on Sandown Road quickly. But still had to end the annual Santa Claus Parade early.

The building and nearby cars suffered significant damages. Thankfully,  no one was injured.
