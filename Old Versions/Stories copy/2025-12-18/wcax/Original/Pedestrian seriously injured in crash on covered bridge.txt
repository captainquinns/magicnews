Pedestrian seriously injured in crash on covered bridge

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-18
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/18/pedestrian-seriously-injured-crash-covered-bridge/

WINDSOR, Vt. (WCAX) - A pedestrian was seriously hurt after being hit by a car on the Windsor-Cornish Covered Bridge.

The Vermont State Police say it happened on Thursday afternoon, shortly after another car crash on nearby Route 12A. Investigators are working to figure out if the two are related.

The pedestrian was brought to Dartmouth Hitchcock with serious injuries.

Police say the driver of the first crash sustained non-life-threatening injuries. Neither has been identified.
