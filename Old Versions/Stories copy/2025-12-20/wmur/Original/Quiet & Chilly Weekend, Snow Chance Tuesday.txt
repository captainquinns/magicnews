Quiet & Chilly Weekend, Snow Chance Tuesday

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-20
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/new-hampshire-weather-121925/69818062

After record warmth on Friday along with gusty winds and rain, the winter chill is back for the rest of the weekend and into next week. The next chance of statewide snow is Tuesday.

>> Interactive Radar

Temperatures were in the 50s Friday afternoon, warm enough for record high temps in Lebanon, Manchester, and Concord, and southerly wind gusts to over 50 mph were recorded in some spots, including a 56 mph gust in Concord.

Tonight will feature clouds and some breaks with a chance of a few flurries in northern areas.

Sunday will feature a blend of sun and clouds along with some flakes at times, mostly in northern NH. It will be quite breezy and seasonably chilly.

Monday will be bright, breezy, and very cold with highs in the 20s in many spots.

The next chance of statewide snow (mostly light to, at times, moderate) will come on Tuesday. Stay tuned to updates over the next 36-48 hours as the exact timeline and snow amounts become more certain.
