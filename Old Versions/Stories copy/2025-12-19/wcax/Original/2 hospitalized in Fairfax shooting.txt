2 hospitalized in Fairfax shooting

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-19
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/19/2-hospitalized-fairfax-shooting/

FAIRFAX, Vt. (WCAX) - Authorities are investigating an early morning shooting in Fairfax that sent two people to the hospital.

Police say they received a 911 call around 1:20 a.m. reporting a shooting at 2 Fletcher Road.

They say the victims, a man and a woman, were taken to Northwestern Medical Center in St. Albans and then transferred to the UVM Medical Center for treatment.

Police say they detained a man at the scene. They say the shooting was an isolated incident and that the people involved knew one another.

Police provided no other immediate details.
