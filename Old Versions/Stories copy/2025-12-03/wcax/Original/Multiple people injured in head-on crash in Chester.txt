Multiple people injured in head-on crash in Chester

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-03
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/03/multiple-injured-after-head-on-collision-chester/

BURLINGTON, Vt. (WCAX) - A head-on collision between a tractor-trailer and an SUV in Chester sent multiple people to the hospital.

It happened at about 10:50 a.m. on Tuesday off Route 103 North near Willard Road.

Both the tractor-trailer and the SUV were left badly damaged, and the people inside had to be extracted.

Chester fire needed help from the police, the Protorsville Fire Department and the Springfield Fire Department to get the people out and clear the scene.

No word yet on what caused the crash or the status of the people taken to the hospital.
