Police seek suspects in Highgate armed robbery

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-03
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/03/police-seek-suspects-highgate-armed-robbery/

HIGHGATE, Vt. (WCAX) - Authorities are investigating an armed robbery at a home in Highgate early Wednesday morning.

It happened at 493 Cook Rd. at about 4 a.m. Vermont State Police say the victims reported that two unidentified people forced their way into the home, threatened them with an unspecified weapon, and stole valuables.

Police described one suspect as a tall, thin man wearing all black with a mask. The other suspect was a short woman wearing a gray coat.

Anyone with information is asked to call the police at 802-524-5993 or provide an anonymous tip online.
