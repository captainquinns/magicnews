Crash temporarily closes Interstate 89 northbound in Georgia

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-03
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/03/crash-closes-interstate-89-northbound-georgia/

GEORGIA, Vt. (WCAX) - A crash temporarily closed Interstate 89 northbound in Georgia on Wednesday.

State officials said at about 4:30 p.m. that the northbound lanes just north of Exit 18 were closed to all traffic due to the crash.

The roadway has since reopened.
