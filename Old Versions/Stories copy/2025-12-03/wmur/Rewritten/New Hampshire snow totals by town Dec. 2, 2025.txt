Winter Weather Arrives in New Hampshire with First Snowfall of the Season

New Hampshire experienced its first snowfall of the season on December 2, 2025, marking the official arrival of winter weather. Town-by-town snow totals have been recorded as of Tuesday evening, although these figures remain preliminary and will be updated as additional reports come in. Any locations not listed indicate no snowfall reports have been submitted, as accumulation can differ significantly within different areas of a town. Residents are encouraged to contribute their snowfall measurements through email if their town is not represented.

Residents are advised to stay informed about weather conditions by downloading the WMUR app, available for both Apple and Android devices. The app offers push notifications for weather alerts specific to users' locations or chosen ZIP codes. It also provides updates on incoming precipitation for local areas. Additionally, viewers can access storm coverage through the free Very Local app on their smart TVs and follow the Storm Watch 9 team across various social media platforms.
