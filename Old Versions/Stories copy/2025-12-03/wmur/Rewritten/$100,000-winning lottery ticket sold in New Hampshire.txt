$100,000 Lottery Ticket Sold in New Hampshire

A ticket for the Tri-State Gimme 5 lottery, valued at $100,000, was purchased in New Hampshire for the drawing held on Tuesday night. Officials from the New Hampshire Lottery reported that the winning ticket was obtained via the NH iLottery app. This ticket correctly matched all five of the drawn numbers: 6, 11, 13, 16, and 26. Tri-State Gimme 5 tickets are available for purchase in Maine, New Hampshire, and Vermont.
