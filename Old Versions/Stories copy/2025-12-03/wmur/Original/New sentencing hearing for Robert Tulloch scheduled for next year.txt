New sentencing hearing for Robert Tulloch scheduled for next year

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-03
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/robert-tulloch-dartmouth-resentencing-april/69623010

The man who murdered two Dartmouth professors when he was 17 will get a new sentencing hearing in April.

Robert Tulloch is serving two concurrent life sentences for killing Half and Susanne Zantop in 2001.

>> Timeline: 2001 murders of Dartmouth College professors; Follow developments in case

In 2012, a U.S. Supreme Court decision banned life sentences without parole for minors.

Tulloch is now scheduled to get a new sentencing hearing on April 20. That's expected to last for four days.
