Headline: Shooting at Jiffy Mart in Walpole Under Investigation

Authorities in Walpole are looking into a shooting incident that occurred Wednesday at a Jiffy Mart located on Main Street. 

At this time, there have been no updates regarding any injuries related to the event. Law enforcement officials have confirmed that they have identified all individuals connected to the shooting and have indicated that there is no ongoing risk to the community. 

Details will be provided as more information becomes available regarding the unfolding situation.
