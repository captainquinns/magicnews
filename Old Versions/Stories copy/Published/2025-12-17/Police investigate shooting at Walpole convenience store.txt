Headline: Authorities Look Into Incident at Walpole Convenience Store

WALPOLE, N.H. - An investigation is currently underway by law enforcement in New Hampshire regarding a shooting that took place at the Jiffy Mart located on Main Street in Walpole on Wednesday. Officers have confirmed that all individuals connected to the incident are located, ensuring there is no ongoing danger to the community. Further details are expected as this situation continues to unfold.
