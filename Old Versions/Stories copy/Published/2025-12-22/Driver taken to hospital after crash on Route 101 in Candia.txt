Headline: Hospitalization follows serious accident on Route 101 in Candia

Emergency responders arrived at the scene of a significant vehicle collision on Route 101 in Candia early Monday. Reports indicate that state troopers were alerted to a car that had gone off the roadway around 6:30 a.m. in the eastbound direction, close to Exit 3. 

The individual driving the vehicle sustained critical injuries and was transported to a medical facility for treatment. Authorities continue to look into the circumstances surrounding the accident.
