Single-vehicle crash on Route 9

Site: REFORMER
Published: 2025-12-23

KEENE, N.H. — Emergency crews from Keene responded to a single-vehicle crash that diverted the westbound lane traffic on Route 9 on top of Chesterfield Hill on Tuesday, Dec. 23, 2025. It's unknown if there were injuries in the crash, which may have been caused by snowy conditions at the time.
