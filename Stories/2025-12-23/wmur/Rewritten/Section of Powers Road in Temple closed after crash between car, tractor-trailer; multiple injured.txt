Headline: Powers Road in Temple Shut Down Following Collision Between Vehicle and Tractor-Trailer

A section of Powers Road in Temple was shut down following a serious accident that involved a car and a tractor-trailer. This incident occurred shortly before 4 p.m. on Tuesday. Several individuals sustained injuries, although the severity of those injuries remains unclear at this time. A News 9 team is present at the location, gathering more details as the situation unfolds. Updates will be provided as new information becomes available.
