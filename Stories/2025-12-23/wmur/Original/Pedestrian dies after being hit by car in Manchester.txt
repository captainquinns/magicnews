Pedestrian dies after being hit by car in Manchester

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-23
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/pedestrian-manchester-south-willow-street-fatal-crash/69847517

Manchester police said a pedestrian died after she was hit by a car Monday evening.

The crash happened on South Willow Street, near the Mall of New Hampshire, at about 5:37 p.m. When officers arrived, they said they located a Mazda CX-9 at the scene. They said the driver, a 52-year-old woman from Nashua, was not injured in the crash.

Authorities said the vehicle was heading northbound on South Willow Street, just north of Huse Road, when the crash happened.

The pedestrian was taken to a local hospital where she was pronounced dead, police said. Her name has not been released, but police said she was 65 years old and from Manchester.

The cause of the crash remains under investigation. Manchester police are asking anyone who may have information to call the traffic unit at 603-668-8711.
