Building destroyed in Springfield fire

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-23
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/springfield-new-hampshire-fire-122325/69850891

Officials in Springfield said a building was destroyed overnight in a fire.

Crews said they got to the scene around 1:30 a.m.

They said that everyone was able to make it out of the building safely.

The cause of the fire has not yet been determined.
