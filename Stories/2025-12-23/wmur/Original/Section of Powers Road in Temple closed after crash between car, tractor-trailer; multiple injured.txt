Section of Powers Road in Temple closed after crash between car, tractor-trailer; multiple injured

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-23
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/powers-road-closed-temple-nh-crash-12232025/69853334

A section of Powers Road in Temple is closed after a serious crash involving a car and a tractor-trailer.

The crash happened just before 4 p.m. Tuesday.

Multiple people were injured, though the extent of their injuries is unknown.

A News 9 crew is on scene working to learn more information.
