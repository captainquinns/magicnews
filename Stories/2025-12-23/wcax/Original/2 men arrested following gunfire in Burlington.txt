2 men arrested following gunfire in Burlington

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-23
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/23/two-men-arrested-following-gunfire-burlington/

BURLINGTON, Vt. (WCAX) - Two people were taken into custody after reported gunshots in Burlington.

Around 9:30 p.m. on Monday, Burlington police responded to North Winooski Ave. and Grant St. for multiple reports of gunshots and people running from the area.

Police collected guns from the scene and arrested Nathan Washington, 46, of Philadelphia, on reckless endangerment and firearms charges.

Shelly Sanders, 42, of Burlington, was also arrested on an unrelated warrant.

Police believe the gunfire was an isolated incident between people who knew each other.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Burlington police at 802-540-2282 or email eschaller@bpdvt.org.
