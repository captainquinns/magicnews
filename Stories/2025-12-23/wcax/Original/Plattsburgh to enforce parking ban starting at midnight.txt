Plattsburgh to enforce parking ban starting at midnight

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-23
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/23/plattsburgh-enforce-parking-ban-starting-midnight/

PLATTSBURGH, N.Y. (WCAX) - Plattsburgh will enforce a citywide parking ban starting at 12:01 a.m. Wednesday ahead of expected snow.

The ban means drivers can’t park on any city streets.

However, there are city parking lots available. Those can be found on the city’s website or by calling 518-536-7461.
