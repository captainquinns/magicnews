Holidays arrive early for the University of Vermont Children’s Hospital

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-16
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/16/holidays-arrive-early-university-vermont-childrens-hospital/

BURLINGTON, Vt. (WCAX) - Patients at the University of Vermont’s Children’s Hospital are in for holiday fun this week.

On Thursday, Santa will deliver all kinds of gifts donated by Vermonters, lending him a helping hand through Operation Fire Cuffs.

Our Sophia Thomas spoke to Timothy Burns and Phil Edgerley of the Burlington Fire Department about what’s in store for Operation Fire Cuffs.
