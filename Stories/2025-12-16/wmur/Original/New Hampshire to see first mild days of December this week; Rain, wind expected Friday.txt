New Hampshire to see first mild days of December this week; Rain, wind expected Friday

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-16
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/new-hampshire-weather-121625/69738858

After the coldest start to December in several years, New Hampshire will finally see some milder temperatures this week.

With the warmer weather comes a likely bout of rain and gusty winds on Friday.

But first, after a more pleasant Tuesday, temps head higher on Wednesday with clouds and partial sun. Highs will top 40 in many parts of central and southern NH.

Sun to clouds on Thursday with highs in the lower half of the 40s for many.

Amid the mild stretch, the precipitation expected Friday will mostly fall in the form of rain for New Hampshire, beginning later Thursday night and during the day on Friday.

The winds will also pick up on Friday morning as temps spike into the 50s for some, with some southerly gusts up to 40 mph possible. Highs will likely warm above 50 degrees in some spots.

Some spots could see up to an inch of rain.

After the rain winds down in the early afternoon hours of Friday, there will be a sharp drop in temperatures along with gusty winds out of the west/northwest late Friday into Saturday.

The winds could dry out roads before any icy spots form for Friday Evening and overnight into early Saturday, but drivers should keep the possibility of slippery travel conditions in mind.

For Saturday, expect fair skies and cooler conditions, with highs in the 30s. A few snow showers possible Saturday Night.

It could be a touch warmer on Sunday, which is the first day of winter. Expect some clouds and partial sun with a chance of a few spotty mixed/snow showers.
