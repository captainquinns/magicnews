Driver freed from rollover crash on Lincoln Hill Road

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-11-30
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/11/30/driver-freed-rollover-crash-lincoln-hill-road/

HINESBURG, Vt. (WCAX) - Officials say a driver sustained minor injuries in a single-vehicle rollover crash early Sunday morning on Lincoln Hill Road.

Hinesburg Fire Department was dispatched at 12:24 a.m. on Nov. 30 for a reported rollover with one person trapped inside the vehicle.

Captain Waite, the incident commander, confirmed upon arrival that the driver was unable to exit the vehicle.

Hinesburg Engine 1 responders used specialized rescue struts to secure the vehicle and then worked to safely free the occupant.

The driver sustained only minor injuries and did not require transport to the hospital.
