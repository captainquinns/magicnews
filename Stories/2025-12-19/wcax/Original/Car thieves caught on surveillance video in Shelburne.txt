Car thieves caught on surveillance video in Shelburne

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-19
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/19/car-thieves-caught-surveillance-video-shelburne/

SHELBURNE, Vt. (WCAX) - Car thieves were caught on surveillance video during an overnight crime spree in Shelburne.

Police say at least two people broke into cars and mailboxes along Ridgefield Road overnight. Police say they stole one car that has since been recovered.

If you live in that area, police want you to review your security cameras and call them with any information.

Police remind residents not to leave anything valuable inside their cars.
