New Hampshire woman arrested after Claremont shooting

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-19
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/19/new-hampshire-woman-arrested-after-claremont-shooting/

CLAREMONT, N.H. (WCAX) - A New Hampshire woman faces charges after a shooting in Claremont Thursday afternoon.

Police say it happened just before 4:30 p.m. near Elm and Terrace Streets.

Officers responded to a call for a person in need of assistance. After securing the area, they later discovered that Leisa Pfenning, 36, was the one who fired the gun and that she had also called police.

She was arrested and faces multiple charges including trespassing, reckless conduct with a deadly weapon, and resisting arrest.

There were no reported injuries.
