Three vehicles involved in Thanksgiving Day crash in Chichester, police say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-11-29
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/nh-chichester-thanksgiving-crash-three-vehicles-11292025/69582112

Chichester Police are investigating a crash from Thursday night involving three vehicles.

Investigators say it happened just before 5 p.m. on Dover Road near Chucksters.

Officials say a car drove into the wrong lane and struck a truck going the opposite direction and then was hit by another vehicle trying to avoid the crash.

It's not clear whether anyone was hurt.

Anyone with information is asked to call Chichester Police.
