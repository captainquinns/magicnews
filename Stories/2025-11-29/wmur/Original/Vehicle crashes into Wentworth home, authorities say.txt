Vehicle crashes into Wentworth home, authorities say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-11-29
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/wentworth-new-hampshire-vehicle-into-house-112925/69581614

A vehicle crashed into a northern New Hampshire home early Saturday morning, authorities said.

Wentworth Fire and EMS said around 3:40 a.m., they were toned for a vehicle into the house, a tree blocking the road and water lines leaking in the house.

The person in the vehicle was removed to the ambulance, authorities said.

The tree was removed from the road and the water problem in the home was taken care of, Wentworth Fire and EMS said.
