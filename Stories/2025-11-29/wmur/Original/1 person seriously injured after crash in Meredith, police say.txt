1 person seriously injured after crash in Meredith, police say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-11-29
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/nh-meredith-crash-serious-injuries-112925/69582306

One person is seriously hurt after a crash in Meredith.

It happened around 6:15 yesterday evening on Route 25. Investigators believe a vehicle crossed over the double yellow line and crashed into other vehicles.'

The driver was taken to the hospital with serious but non-life-threatening injuries

One other driver had minor injuries.

The cause of the crash is still under investigation.
