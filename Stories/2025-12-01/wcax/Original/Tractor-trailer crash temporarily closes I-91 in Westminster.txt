Tractor-trailer crash temporarily closes I-91 in Westminster

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-01
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/01/tractor-trailer-crash-closes-portion-interstate-91/

WESTMINSTER, Vt. (WCAX) - A tractor-trailer crash on Interstate 91 Monday morning temporarily closed the southbound lanes of the highway.

The closure ran from Exit 5 in Westminster to Exit 4 in Putney after the truck crashed into a guardrail at mile marker 19.6.

The roadway has since mostly reopened, though the left southbound lane was closed so crews could repair the guardrail.
