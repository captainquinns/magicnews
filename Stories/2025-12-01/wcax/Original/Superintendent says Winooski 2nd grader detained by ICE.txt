Superintendent says Winooski 2nd grader detained by ICE

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-01
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/01/superintendent-says-winooski-2nd-grader-detained-by-ice/

WINOOSKI, Vt. (WCAX) - A second grader in the Winooski School District was taken into immigration custody with his mother while traveling during school vacation. That’s according to the district’s superintendent .

The superintendent is calling for the student’s immediate release and calling on the U.S. government to bring basic humanity and due process back.

WCAX News is working to gather more details on this story.
