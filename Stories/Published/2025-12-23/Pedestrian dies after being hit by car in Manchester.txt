Headline: Tragic Incident Claims Life of Pedestrian in Manchester

A pedestrian's life was lost after being struck by a vehicle on Monday night in Manchester. The incident occurred at approximately 5:37 p.m. on South Willow Street, close to the Mall of New Hampshire. Upon their arrival, law enforcement officials identified a Mazda CX-9 involved in the event. The driver, a 52-year-old resident of Nashua, was reported to be unharmed.

Investigators indicated that the vehicle was traveling north on South Willow Street, just north of Huse Road, during the collision. The pedestrian, a 65-year-old woman from Manchester, was transported to a nearby hospital but ultimately succumbed to her injuries. Authorities have yet to disclose her name as they continue their inquiry.

An investigation into the circumstances surrounding this accident is ongoing, and Manchester police are encouraging any witnesses or individuals with further details to reach out to the traffic unit at 603-668-8711.
