Headline: Route 9 Sees Disruption Due to Solo Vehicle Accident

Emergency services in Keene attended to a solo vehicle accident that occurred on Chesterfield Hill on Route 9, impacting westbound traffic. The incident took place on Tuesday, December 23, 2025. At this time, authorities have not confirmed whether any individuals sustained injuries. Preliminary assessments suggest that poor weather conditions, including snowfall, may have played a role in the mishap.
