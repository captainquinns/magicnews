Mercedes-Benz Settles Diesel Emissions Controversy with Multiple States, Including New Hampshire

Mercedes-Benz has come to an agreement with a group of states, among them New Hampshire, in connection with a scandal regarding diesel emissions. The involved states had charged the company with misleading consumers regarding the pollution levels of its vehicles in advertising and during emissions testing. As part of this resolution, the automaker will face financial penalties amounting to close to $150 million.
