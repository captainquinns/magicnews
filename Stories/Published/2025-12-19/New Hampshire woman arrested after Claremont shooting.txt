Headline: Woman for Claremont Shooting Taken into Custody

A shooting incident that unfolded in Claremont, New Hampshire, during the afternoon on Thursday has led to the arrest of a local woman. The event occurred shortly before 4:30 p.m. at the intersection of Elm and Terrace Streets.

Authorities were alerted to the scene after receiving a report regarding an individual requiring help. Upon arrival and securing the location, law enforcement identified 36-year-old Leisa Pfenning as the shooter, who had also contacted the police herself.

Pfenning now faces several legal charges, including reckless conduct with a deadly weapon, trespassing, and resisting arrest. Fortunately, no injuries were reported as a result of the incident.
