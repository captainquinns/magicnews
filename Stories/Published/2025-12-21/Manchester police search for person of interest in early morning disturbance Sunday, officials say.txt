Headline: Manchester Police Seek Help Following Morning Incident at Police Station

Authorities in Manchester are reaching out to the public for information regarding an incident that occurred in the early hours of Sunday at the police department's lobby. A male individual was reported to have walked into the headquarters and expressed intentions of causing harm, displaying signs of agitation. 

He exited the lobby prior to being recognized. Subsequently, law enforcement has reportedly managed to identify and locate this individual, bringing a measure of resolution to the situation.
