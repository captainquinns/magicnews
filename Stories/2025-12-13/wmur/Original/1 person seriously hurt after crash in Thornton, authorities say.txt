1 person seriously hurt after crash in Thornton, authorities say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-13
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/nh-one-person-seriously-hurt-after-crash-in-thornton-authorities-say-121325/69717679

One person was seriously injured after a crash in Thornton, Friday evening.

Just before 7:30 p.m. Friday, first responders got a report of a crash with someone trapped on Interstate 93 South.

It took crews around 20 minutes to get the person out.

They were taken to a local hospital.

State Police are working to figure out what caused the crash.
