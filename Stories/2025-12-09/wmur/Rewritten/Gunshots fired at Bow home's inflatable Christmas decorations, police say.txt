Headline: Investigation Underway After Gunshots Target Holiday Decorations in Bow

Police are requesting assistance from the community in identifying the individuals responsible for shooting at festive decorations outside a residence early on Tuesday morning. 

Around 1 a.m. on Tuesday, several inflatable Christmas figures were damaged by gunfire at a house located on Risingwood Drive in Bow. Authorities believe the shots originated from the street, indicating that the shooter or shooters may have been either in a vehicle or on foot near the roadway.

Officials emphasized that this vandalism not only harms private property but also poses significant risks to the safety of local residents. Depending on the outcome of the investigation, charges related to criminal mischief and firearm-related reckless conduct may be sought against any identified suspects.

Fortunately, no injuries were reported as a result of the incident. 

Local authorities are urging residents along Risingwood Drive and surrounding streets to check their home security cameras for any footage captured between 1 a.m. and 2 a.m. on Tuesday, specifically looking for any unusual vehicles or suspects.

Individuals with any relevant information are encouraged to get in touch with Lt. Matt Pratte at 603-223-3950. Anonymous tips can also be submitted through an online link.
