Vermont man dies while serving as combat medic in Ukraine

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-09
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/09/vermont-man-dies-while-serving-combat-medic-ukraine/

BRATTLEBORO, Vt. (WCAX) - A Vermonter has died in combat in Ukraine.

A mother and father in Brattleboro are grieving the loss of their son, Andrew David Mario, 33. They described him as “deeply compassionate with a fiery passion for social justice.”

Mario was killed in action while in combat operations on Dec. 3. He was serving as a medic with the International Brigade of the Ukrainian National Guard at the time.

A memorial service is being planned for spring in Newfane.
