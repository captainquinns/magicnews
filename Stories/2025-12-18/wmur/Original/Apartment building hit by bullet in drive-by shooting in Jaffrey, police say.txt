Apartment building hit by bullet in drive-by shooting in Jaffrey, police say

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-18
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/drive-by-shooting-jaffrey-121825/69810452

An investigation is underway in Jaffrey after a drive-by shooting Wednesday evening at an apartment building.

Police said gunshots were reported at about 5 p.m. in the area of Cross Street and Nutting Road.

Investigators said shots were fired from a vehicle as it passed the apartment building. One bullet penetrated an unoccupied apartment, police said.

No injuries were reported.

Police said they believe the shooting was a targeted incident.
