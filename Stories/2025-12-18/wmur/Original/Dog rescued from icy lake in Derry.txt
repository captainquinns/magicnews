Dog rescued from icy lake in Derry

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-18
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/derry-new-hampshire-dog-pug-rescue/69807923

A dog is safe after being rescued in Derry.

Firefighters were called to help the pug after she got stuck on the ice on the Beaver Lake outlet on Wednesday.

Crews said the pug, named Bella, was chilly but otherwise OK.

She was reunited with her family a short time later.
