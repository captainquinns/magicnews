Professional chefs invited to compete in NH Food Bank's Steel Chef Challenge

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-22
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/apply-nh-food-bank-steel-chef-challenge/69841859

Culinary professionals in New Hampshire are encouraged to apply to be a part of the New Hampshire Food Bank's Steel Chef Challenge.

The annual event is in its 10th year. It features timed cooking competitions with all proceeds benefiting the New Hampshire Food Bank.

The deadline to apply is Jan. 31, 2026. See the application here .
