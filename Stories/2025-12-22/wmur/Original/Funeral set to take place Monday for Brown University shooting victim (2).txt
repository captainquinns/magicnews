Funeral set to take place Monday for Brown University shooting victim

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-22
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/brown-university-ella-cook-shooting-funeral/69837878

The funeral for one of the victims of the Brown University shooting is set to take place on Monday.

Ella Cook will be remembered in her home state of Alabama.

She was a sophomore at Brown, studying economics and French. Police said two other people died at the hands of the same shooter.

Brown University freshman Mukhammad Aziz Umurzokov was killed.

Two days later, MIT professor Nuno Loureiro was killed in his home in Brookline, Massachusetts.
