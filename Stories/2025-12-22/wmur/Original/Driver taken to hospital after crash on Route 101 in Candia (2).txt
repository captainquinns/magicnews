Driver taken to hospital after crash on Route 101 in Candia

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-22
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/candia-new-hampshire-crash-122225/69840167

Emergency crews responded to a serious crash on Route 101 in Candia on Monday morning.

State troopers said they got a call about a vehicle off the road at around 6:30 a.m. by the eastbound lane near Exit 3.

The driver of the car was taken to a hospital with life-threatening injuries.

The crash is still under investigation.
