Fire crews extinguish blaze at Londonderry restaurant

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-02
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/02/fire-crews-extinguish-blaze-londonderry-restaurant/

LONDONDERRY, Vt. (WCAX) - Crews responded early Tuesday morning to a fire at a restaurant in Londonderry.

It happened around 4:40 a.m. at the Revival Kitchen restaurant on Route 11.

Officials say the building was empty and no one was injured. Crews had the fire out by around 6:45 a.m.

The cause of the fire, as well as the extent of the damage, was not immediately available.
