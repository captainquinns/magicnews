Headline: Three Individuals Hospitalized After Fire Engulfs Multiple Buildings in Nashua

A fire erupted early Tuesday in Nashua, affecting at least two structures. The incident began shortly before 6:30 a.m. in the vicinity of Ash and Vine streets. According to an officer present at the scene, the flames initiated from a six-family residence located at 108 Ash Street and later extended to a warehouse at 115 Vine Street. Law enforcement collaborated with firefighters to ensure the safe evacuation of all residents from the apartment complex, and they are confident that everyone was removed from the premises. Emergency services reported that three individuals were taken to nearby hospitals for treatment of injuries that were not considered life-threatening.
