Headline: Investigators on the Hunt for Armed Robbery Suspects in Highgate

Authorities in Highgate, Vermont, are currently looking into a home invasion that took place in the early hours of Wednesday. The incident occurred around 4 a.m. at a residence located at 493 Cook Rd. According to reports from the Vermont State Police, the victims mentioned that two unidentified individuals entered their home forcibly, brandished an unknown weapon, and made off with valuable items.

Law enforcement officials have provided a description of the suspects: one is a tall, slender male dressed entirely in black and wearing a mask, while the other is a shorter female clad in a gray coat. Police are appealing to the public for any leads and encourage anyone with information to reach out at 802-524-5993 or to submit tips anonymously online.
