State police issue 13,000+ tickets over Thanksgiving holiday

Site: WCAX
Published: 2025-12-03
URL: https://www.wcax.com/2025/12/03/state-police-issue-13000-tickets-over-thanksgiving-holiday/

ALBANY, New York (WWNY) - State police issued more than 13,000 tickets across New York as drivers traveled for the Thanksgiving holiday.

Troopers began the enforcement initiative last Wednesday and continued through Sunday.

Throughout the campaign, police conducted sobriety checkpoints, deployed additional DWI patrols, and ticketed drivers who were using handheld electronic devices.

They also focused on speeding and aggressive driving.

Troopers issued 13,036 tickets across the state.

Police arrested 247 people for DWI and investigated 1,277 crashes, including two fatal crashes.
