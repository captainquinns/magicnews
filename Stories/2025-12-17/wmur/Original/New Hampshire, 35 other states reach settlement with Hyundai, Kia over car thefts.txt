New Hampshire, 35 other states reach settlement with Hyundai, Kia over car thefts

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/new-hampshire-settlement-hyundai-kia-car-thefts/69796895

New Hampshire is part of a settlement with Hyundai and Kia over vehicle thefts.

The lawsuit was filed by a group of attorneys general over the lack of anti-theft technology in those vehicles.

Tens of thousands of cars have been stolen across the country.

As part of the settlement , the automakers agreed to equip all future vehicles with software upgrades, offer free ignition protectors for people with older models and provide $4.5 million in restitution to eligible owners.

The states will also receive $4.5 million for investigation costs.
