Driver killed in crash in Hooksett

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/hooksett-new-hampshire-crash-121725/69797773

A driver was killed in a two-car crash on Wednesday morning in Hooksett.

The crash occurred around 7:15 a.m. on Route 3A, which is also known as West River Road.

Two people in the other car were taken to a hospital with unknown injuries, police said.

The identity of the crash victim was not released.

A section of Route 3A, between Cross Road and Hackett Hill Road, was closed for some time but was back open before 1 p.m.
