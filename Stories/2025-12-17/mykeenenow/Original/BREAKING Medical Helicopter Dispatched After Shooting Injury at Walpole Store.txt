WALPOLE, N.H. (MyKeeneNow) Emergency crews responded late Wednesday morning to a reported traumatic injury involving a gunshot wound at Jiffy Mart on Main Street in Walpole, according to emergency dispatch information.

The incident was reported shortly before 11 a.m. Initial reports indicated a victim suffering from a gunshot wound. Due to the severity of the injury, air medical transport was requested.

An update from emergency officials confirmed that DHART was en route to the helipad at Cheshire Medical Center to assist with the patient’s transport and treatment.

No additional details about the victim’s condition or the circumstances surrounding the incident were immediately available.

Walpole Police confirmed there is no threat to the public at this time.

This is a developing story and will be updated as more information is released.
