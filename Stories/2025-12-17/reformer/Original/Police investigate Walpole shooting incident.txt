Police investigate Walpole shooting incident

Site: REFORMER
Published: 2025-12-17
URL: https://www.reformer.com/local-news/police-investigate-walpole-shooting-incident/article_458fb1e7-869f-4f8a-8ad6-df98862c9f59.html

WALPOLE, N.H. — Police officers from Walpole, the New Hampshire State Police and Bellows Falls in Vermont responded to a call Wednesday morning about a person with a gunshot wound on Route 12.

Police quickly set up a perimeter around the Jiffy Mart as they investigated a shooting that happened just before 11 a.m. One person was transported to Cheshire Medical Center in Keene, N.H., for what was described as a trauma injury. A medical helicopter was called to the medical center for the shooting victim.

Walpole Police and New Hampshire State Police are investigating a shooting incident at Jiffy Mart in Walpole, N.H. on Route 12. Police report no danger to the community. One person was transported for injuries to Cheshire Medical Center in Keene, N.H.

A Facebook post by the Walpole Police Department stated that all individuals involved have been accounted for and there is no threat to the public.

Police were seen photographing a red pickup truck with the gas pump still connected to the vehicle’s gas tank. Also seen was a blue pickup truck that police were photographing as well, which had its door open.

No other information is available at this time.
