Salem police arrest Nashua woman in connection with robbery

Site: WMUR
Published: 2025-12-12
URL: https://www.wmur.com/article/jennifer-young-salem-gas-station-robbery-12112025/69703150

Salem police arrested a Nashua woman Thursday after she allegedly robbed a store.

Authorities said the robbery happened shortly after 5 a.m. at the 7-Eleven on Cluff Crossing Road. Police said the suspect ran off with the cash register.

Officers responding to the area stopped a vehicle leaving the scene and detained the driver, identified as 42-year-old Jennifer Young.

Police said the investigation and physical evidence led to her arrest.

Young faces multiple charges, including robbery.
